Teas

I enjoy tea and was able to do a tea tasting recently. It turns out that all different types of tea (not herbal) come from the same plant, the Camellia sinesis. This includes black tea, green tea, white tea, and oolong tea. The difference in flavor comes from where the leaf was grown and the oxidation level it has from the drying process. To learn more about the tea processing method visit the link here. I also like drinking a red tea called rooibos tea, but this is an herbal tea that comes from a different plant.
